## What Happened
Fixed critical audio bug where match hype chimes and drop sounds overlapped and cut off after each match event.

## Root Cause
Per-tile sound playback from `Tile.MoveTile()` caused `AudioStreamPlayer` exhaustion under rapid batched dispatch (5–15 tiles/frame).

## Resolution
Consolidated sound triggering to `GameBoard._Process()` with round-based guards. Fixed off-by-one in hype level array access.

## Files Modified
- `Scenes/GameBoard.cs` (sound logic + guards)
- `Core/GameObjects/Tile.cs` (removed per-tile calls)
